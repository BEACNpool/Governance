### UNKNOWN
- A decisive CF Byron→Shelley bridge receipt remains unresolved in current public-bounded outputs.

## What this does NOT mean
- It does **not** mean CF founder-era funds are disproven, exonerated, or fully mapped.
- It does **not** mean the overall project failed.
- It does mean public-data-only bounded tracing has current limits on unresolved founder flows.

## Current project conclusion
The project is building an evidence-first public record of what can be proven, what can be strongly inferred, and what remains unknown. The current phase did not fully resolve all founder genesis ADA destinations, but it materially narrowed uncertainty and clarified the present limits of bounded public-data tracing.
